Philippe Coutinho

About Philippe Coutinho

Philippe Coutinho is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or winger for Aston Villa and the Brazil national team.

Born on June 12, 1992, in Rio de Janeiro, Coutinho started his professional career at Vasco da Gama before moving to Europe to join Inter Milan in 2008. He later played for Liverpool FC, where he became known for his exceptional dribbling, vision, and ability to score goals from long range.

Coutinho joined Barcelona in January 2018 in a transfer worth over £100 million, making him one of the most expensive players in football history at the time. He has also represented Brazil at international tournaments, winning the Copa America in 2019.

Coutinho is known for his versatility, creativity, and skillful playmaking ability, making him a valuable asset for both club and country.
