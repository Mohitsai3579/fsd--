Mauro Icardi

About Mauro Icardi

Mauro Icardi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a striker. Known for his goal-scoring prowess and poacher's instinct, Icardi is one of the most lethal finishers in the game.

Icardi began his professional career at Sampdoria before moving to Inter Milan, where he established himself as one of Serie A's top strikers. During his time at Inter Milan, Icardi won the Serie A Golden Boot and served as the club's captain.

Icardi has also represented the Argentine national team, earning caps and scoring crucial goals for his country. He is known for his ability to find space in the box, his aerial prowess, and his clinical finishing ability with both feet.
