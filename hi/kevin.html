Kevin De Bruyne

About Kevin De Bruyne

Kevin De Bruyne is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Manchester City and the Belgian national team. Known for his exceptional passing, vision, and versatility, De Bruyne is considered one of the best midfielders in the world.

De Bruyne began his professional career at Genk in Belgium before moving to Chelsea and later to Wolfsburg in Germany. He joined Manchester City in 2015, where he has won multiple Premier League titles and other domestic honors.

De Bruyne is known for his ability to dictate the tempo of the game, his precise crosses, and his long-range shooting. He has also represented Belgium at international tournaments, helping his country achieve success at the FIFA World Cup and the UEFA European Championship.
