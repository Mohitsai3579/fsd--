Harry Kane

About Harry Kane

Harry Kane is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker for Tottenham Hotspur and the England national team. Known for his goal-scoring ability, leadership, and work rate, Kane is considered one of the best strikers in the world.

Kane began his professional career at Tottenham Hotspur, where he rose through the youth ranks to become the club's all-time leading goal scorer. He has won the Premier League Golden Boot multiple times and has been named the PFA Team of the Year on several occasions.

Kane has also represented England at international tournaments, including the FIFA World Cup and the UEFA European Championship. He is known for his ability to score goals with both feet, his aerial prowess, and his proficiency from set-pieces.
