Mesut Özil

About Mesut Özil

Mesut Özil is a German professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder. Known for his vision, technique, and playmaking ability, Özil is widely regarded as one of the best assist providers in the game.

Özil began his professional career at Schalke 04 before moving to Werder Bremen and then to Real Madrid, where he won La Liga and Copa del Rey titles. In 2013, he transferred to Arsenal in the English Premier League, where he became a fan favorite and established himself as one of the top players in the league.

Özil has also represented the German national team, winning the FIFA World Cup in 2014. He is known for his calmness on the ball, precise passing, and ability to unlock defenses with his creativity.
